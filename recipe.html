here is how to make a normal cookie!

the ingredients

120 grams of butter

1 egg

half cup brown sugar

half cup white sugar

1 pinch salt

1 tablespoon baking powder

1 tablespoon vanilla sugar

3 cups of all purpose flour

now, put all of these ingredients to a bowl and mix them up like a couple of minutes!"

once it turns into a dough you can shape them anything you want! like a square or even the world!

if your done shaping the cookies, you can bake them in an oven. also its 375 degrees fahrenheit

now wait! if you see the dough has turn cooked, then its your time to remove it from the oven!

and there you have it! your own normal cookies!
